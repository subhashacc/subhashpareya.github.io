Who I Am

About Me

I am a Finance & Accounts Professional based in Bangalore with over 15 years of total professional experience, including 6+ years of deep-core accounting expertise across NGO, corporate, and educational sectors.

My journey spans roles in financial reporting, accounts payable & receivable, general ledger, compliance, and ERP implementation. I bring a unique cross-sector perspective — having worked with international standards (US GAAP, IFRS) alongside Indian regulatory frameworks (GST, TDS, Income Tax, Professional Tax).

Currently at CEE South, Bangalore, I manage end-to-end finance operations, statutory compliance, and project accounting for development sector programmes. I am actively pursuing US Accounting & Bookkeeping Certification and self-learning SAP FICO to expand my global finance capabilities.
